Star Wars Episode VII Teaser is just plain boring

I love Star Wars probably more than half of you can probably know. I grew up watching Star Wars on VHS and wondering how those two movies, Star Wars and Empire Strikes Back could end so tragically. I remember the day that my parents left a copy of Return of the Jedi (probably in 1986) in the house and I watch it multiple times back to back that day. I couldn't believe what I had seen. By the time I was 10, I could recite every line of Return of the Jedi from beginning to end. I remember paying a friend to buy me re-release tickets in 1997. I couldn't camp out for them because I was still in school. I also remember in 1999 when I was a senior in high school and my parents actually let me skip school to camp out for Star Wars Episode I tickets. I saw Episode I in theater more than 27 times that summer. I remember taking my daughter to the theater to see Episode I in 3D in 2012.

I love Star Wars. I never demanded that Star Wars fit my idea of what it was. I was happy to explore George Lucas's imagination. I did not condemn Ewoks as I know some had. I did not loathe Jar Jar to the point where I ruined the whole experience for myself and everyone around me like many of you did.

As an aside, my love for Star Wars is mostly limited to the films though. I love Lucasfilm Games/LucasArts games but I never fell in love with their Star Wars story lines. I never fell in love with the books or comics. The TV shows were enjoyable, but I hardly loved them.

But back to my point, my criticism of the Star Wars Episode VII trailer is hardly the criticism of a cynic who just loves to hate things. I was never the cynic condemning everything that wasn't The Empire Strikes Back, as many of you do. This is an objective criticism of a poorly produced teaser.
