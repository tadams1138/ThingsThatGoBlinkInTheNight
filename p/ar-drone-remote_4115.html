AR Drone Remote

Turn your Windows 8 system, surface or Windows phone into a remote control for your Parrot brand AR Drone!



How To Use It:

  1. First connect your phone to the drone's wifi
  2. Touch the Connect button on the AR Drone Remote app menu bar
  3. Once the app connects to the drone (you will know when you can see battery power or other indicators changing)
  4. Touch the Take Off/Land button to start the engines.
  5. Move the red ball on the left to fly the drone forward, backwards, left and right.
  6. Move the red ball on the right to fly the drone up or down or to turn it left or right.
  7. When you are done, touch the Take Off/Land button to return the drone safely back to the ground.
  8. If you absolutely need to, you can immediately cut the engines by touching the Emergency button. Once you have safely retrieved your drone, you will have to touch the Emergency button again to release your drone from Emergency mode.

Keyboard mapping:

  • W - Forward
  • A - Left
  • S - Backward
  • D -Right
  • Up Arrow - Up
  • Left Arrow - Turn Left
  • Down Arrow - Down
  • Right Arrow - Turn Right
